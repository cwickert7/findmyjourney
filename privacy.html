How we handle your information — clearly and honestly.

Last updated: May 2026

Short version: Find My Journey does not store your assessment answers beyond your current session. We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described below.

Short version: Find My Journey collects only what is necessary to deliver your results. We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described below.

1. Who we are

Find My Journey is an AI-powered career discovery tool for young Australians aged 15–25. We are operated by Find My Journey (ABN 94 672 651 253), a sole trader business registered in Victoria, Australia. Our contact email is hello@findmyjourney.com.au.

2. What information we collect

Assessment answers

When you use the Find My Journey assessment, you answer questions about your interests, values, personality and work preferences. These answers are used solely to generate your personalised career results. If you choose to save your results, your raw assessment answers are stored securely in our database and retained for 12 months. They are not used for any purpose other than enabling you to return to your results.

Name and location

We ask for your first name and optionally your state and city. Your name is used to personalise your results. Your location is used to surface locally relevant course providers and pathways.

Email address (optional)

If you choose to receive your results by email, we collect your email address. We use this only to send you your results and return link. We do not use this to send marketing emails.

Usage data

We collect anonymous usage data including pages visited, features used and general session information via Google Analytics. This data cannot be linked to an individual and is used solely to improve Find My Journey.

Saved results

If you choose to save your results, we store the following securely for 12 months:

  • Your first name
  • Your raw assessment answers (question and answer pairs, including question text at time of completion)
  • Your career matches (job titles and summaries)
  • Your career branch
  • Whether the Sensitive Explorer Protocol was activated
  • Your state and city (if provided)
  • Your institution or campus ID (if applicable)
  • The date your results were generated

After 12 months, saved results are permanently and automatically deleted.

Institution-linked assessments

Where you complete an assessment through an institutional arrangement (for example, through your school, TAFE, or employment service provider), your results are also accessible to authorised staff at that institution through the Find My Journey portal. In this context, the institution is the primary data controller for your information, and Find My Journey acts as a data processor on its behalf.

3. How we use your information

  • To generate your personalised career results and CV
  • To send you your results if you request email delivery
  • To store your results for 12 months if you choose to save them
  • To share your results with your institution's authorised advisors, if you are completing an institution-linked assessment
  • To surface locally relevant course providers and pathways based on your state and city
  • To improve the quality and accuracy of Find My Journey over time
  • To understand how the tool is being used at an aggregate level

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling or any commercial purpose beyond operating Find My Journey.

4. Who we share your information with

Anthropic

Your assessment answers are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your results. Anthropic processes this data as a service provider under their privacy policy. Your answers are not used to train Anthropic's AI models.

Cloud database provider

If you save your results, or if you complete an institution-linked assessment, your data is stored in a secure cloud database hosted in Sydney, Australia. This provider acts as a data processor on our behalf and does not access your data for any purpose other than storage and retrieval.

Cache and storage provider

Your results return link is stored in a secure cache and storage service hosted in Sydney, Australia, and retained for 12 months. This provider acts as a data processor on our behalf.

Email delivery provider

If you request email delivery of your results, your email address and results summary are sent to a transactional email delivery provider to deliver the email. This provider does not retain your data beyond delivery.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data about how visitors interact with our site. This data is aggregated and cannot be linked to an individual. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Your institution (if applicable)

If you complete an assessment through an institutional arrangement, your results are accessible to authorised staff at that institution. Find My Journey does not control how institutions use this information — that is governed by the institution's own privacy policies and their agreement with Find My Journey.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with any other third parties.

5. Parental consent and under-18s

Find My Journey is designed for young Australians aged 15–25. We encourage anyone under 15 to let a parent or guardian know they are using this tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Where Find My Journey is used through an institutional arrangement involving students under 18, the institution is responsible for ensuring that appropriate parental or guardian consent has been obtained in accordance with applicable laws and the institution's own policies.

6. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse or disclosure. Our databases are hosted in Australia and protected by industry-standard security measures including row-level security, encrypted connections and access controls. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage system is completely secure.

7. Data retention

  • Assessment answers (unsaved sessions): Not retained after your session ends
  • Saved results: Retained for 12 months then permanently deleted
  • Institution-linked results: Retained in accordance with the institution's agreement with Find My Journey
  • Email send records: Retained for 12 months
  • Anonymous usage data: Retained in accordance with Google Analytics retention settings

8. Your rights

Under Australian privacy law (Privacy Act 1988) you have the right to access, correct or request deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@findmyjourney.com.au.

You may request early deletion of your saved results at any time by contacting us with your personal return link or result code.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when we do. Continued use of Find My Journey after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@findmyjourney.com.au.

Find My Journey is an AI-powered career discovery tool for young Australians aged 15–25. We are operated by Find My Journey (ABN placeholder). Our contact email is hello@findmyjourney.com.au.

2. What information we collect

Assessment answers

When you use the Find My Journey assessment, you answer questions about your interests, values, personality and work preferences. These answers are used solely to generate your personalised career results. They are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers after your session ends.

Email address (optional)

If you choose to receive your results by email, we collect your email address. We use this only to send you your results. We store a record that an email was sent (email address, date, job titles) in a secure database to help us understand usage. We do not use this to send marketing emails.

Usage data

We collect anonymous usage data including pages visited, features used, and general session information. This data cannot be linked to an individual and is used solely to improve Find My Journey.

Feedback

If you use the thumbs up/down feedback buttons, we record an anonymised positive or negative signal alongside the career branch selected. No personal information is collected with this feedback.

Saved results (optional)

If you choose to save your results, we store the following information securely for 12 months:

  • Your first name
  • Your career matches (job titles and summaries)
  • Whether the Sensitive Explorer Protocol was activated
  • Your institution or program code, if you provided one
  • The date your results were generated

We do not store your raw assessment answers as part of saved results. After 12 months, saved results are permanently and automatically deleted.

If you are under 15, we recommend letting a parent or guardian know that your results are being saved.

3. How we use your information

  • To generate your personalised career results and CV
  • To send you your results if you request email delivery
  • To store your results for 12 months if you choose to save them, so you can return and explore further
  • To share your results with your institution or program pathways advisor, if you provided an institution code
  • To improve the quality and accuracy of Find My Journey over time
  • To understand how the tool is being used at an aggregate level

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling or any commercial purpose beyond operating Find My Journey.

3a. Saved results — consent and storage

Saving your results is entirely optional. When you choose to save, we will clearly notify you that your results are being stored, what is stored, and for how long. You will receive a personal link to retrieve your results at any time within the 12-month storage period.

If you entered an institution or program code during your assessment, your saved results will be accessible to the authorised pathways advisors at that institution. Only results from Explorers who actively provided an institution code are visible to that institution — institutions cannot access results from Explorers who did not opt in to their program.

You may request early deletion of your saved results at any time by contacting us at hello@findmyjourney.com.au with your personal link or result code.

4. Who we share your information with

Anthropic

Your assessment answers are sent to Anthropic’s Claude API to generate your results. Anthropic processes this data as a service provider under their privacy policy. Your answers are not used to train Anthropic’s AI models.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with any other third parties.

5. Under 15s

Find My Journey is designed for young Australians aged 15–25. We encourage anyone under 15 to let a parent or guardian know they are using this tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your rights

Under Australian privacy law (Privacy Act 1988) you have the right to access, correct or request deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@findmyjourney.com.au.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Assessment answers are never written to persistent storage. Email addresses are stored in a secured, access-controlled database.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.

9. Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email us at hello@findmyjourney.com.au.
