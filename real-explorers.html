The Sensitive Explorer Protocol

Built for those who need
a different approach.

For young people with anxiety, learning differences, autism spectrum conditions or disability — Find My Journey quietly adapts without them having to disclose anything.

🤟
No labels required
The assessment detects signals of sensitivity through how someone answers — and responds accordingly. No diagnosis needed. No boxes to tick.
🧘
Calm career matches
When the Sensitive Explorer Protocol activates, results are filtered toward calm, structured, predictable roles — and away from high-sensory or unpredictable environments.
👥
Advisor visibility
Pathways advisors see when SEP has been triggered — so they can prepare for the session with the right approach before the young person walks in the door.
