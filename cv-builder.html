What makes it different

Not a template.
A real reflection of you.

The Find My Journey CV Builder isn’t a form you fill in and format. It’s a conversation that becomes a document.

🧠
Pre-filled from your assessment
Complete the career discovery and your name is pre-filled, your branch and personality profile are translated into genuine professional skills, and your Explorer narrative shapes how Claude writes your profile summary. No blank page.
✏️
AI writes your profile summary
We generate a personal profile statement in plain, honest language — not buzzword-filled corporate speak. Sounds like a person, not a template.
🇦🇺
Australian jobs and context
Built for the Australian job market. Australian spelling, Australian role titles, Australian pathways. Not a US or UK tool retrofitted for here.
🤝
Works for everyone
Designed to be calm, low-pressure and accessible. No timed sections, no confusing forms. Works especially well for young people with learning differences or anxiety.
📋
Paste into any application
Plain text output that works everywhere — Seek, LinkedIn, email, Google Docs. No proprietary format that only works in one place.
🔒
Private and secure
Your CV content is yours. We don’t store it beyond your session unless you choose to save your results. No selling your data. No ads.
