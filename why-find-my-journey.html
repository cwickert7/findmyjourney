Four proof points

Not another quiz.
Something different.

Most career tools were built for institutions. Find My Journey was built for the young person sitting in front of it. Here’s what makes it genuinely different.

🧠
Built on real psychology
Find My Journey draws on Holland’s occupational codes, Super’s life-span theory and Savickas’s career construction framework — the same foundations used by professional career counsellors worldwide. Adapted for Australian young people aged 15–25, not borrowed from a US or UK context.
Sources: Holland (1997), Super (1990), Savickas (2011), positive psychology (Seligman)
🤖
AI that actually personalises
The results aren’t a database lookup. Claude — Anthropic’s AI — reads your complete profile and generates a career narrative built around you specifically. The “why this fits you” explanation for each career match is written fresh, every time, for every Explorer.
Powered by Anthropic’s Claude — the same AI used by leading research institutions globally
🤝
Genuinely inclusive by design
The Sensitive Explorer Protocol detects when a young person may benefit from a calmer, more structured experience — without them having to disclose a diagnosis. No timed sections, no competitive framing, no jargon. Designed to work for neurodiverse young people from the ground up, not retrofitted afterward.
Informed by Dr. Chen’s developmental psychology framework and DES provider feedback
🇦🇺
Australian — properly
Australian career titles. Australian salary data. Australian training pathways — TAFE, RTOs, apprenticeships, university. Australian spelling. Built for the Australian job market and calibrated for Australian conditions. Not a US product with Australian logos stuck on top.
Career data sourced from ANZSCO, SEEK and Australian Government Labour Market Insights
How we compare

What others don’t
do.

Career tools have been around for decades. Here’s what makes Find My Journey a genuine step forward — not just a newer version of the same thing.

💭
Whole-person profiling
Most tools ask about skills and qualifications. We ask about personality, values, work style and environment preferences — because that’s what actually predicts whether someone will be happy and stay.
⚡
No practitioner required
Tools like JIIG-CAL’s Career Voyage require accredited adviser administration. Find My Journey is fully self-directed — a young person can use it alone, at home, at midnight. Advisors are optional, not mandatory.
💬
Honest Hope philosophy
We don’t tell young people what they should do. We tell them what genuinely fits who they are — and we’re honest about the pathway required. No false promises, no generic encouragement.
📊
Real-time advisor data
The institution dashboard gives pathways advisors live visibility into their cohort — who’s completed, who’s flagged for sensitive support, what career clusters are emerging — before the session even starts.
🔄
Return and explore
Results are saved for 12 months. Explorers can return, re-explore, and build on their results over time — not a one-shot assessment but an ongoing discovery process.
📱
Mobile-first, no account
No app download, no account creation, no login. Works on any device, any browser. The lowest possible barrier to a young person who’s never used a career tool before.
